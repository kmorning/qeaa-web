Open and Closed Meeting Formats

When you browse our meeting list, you'll find that meetings are designated either as Open or Closed format. Anyone may attend an Open meeting of A.A. This includes friends and family of an alcoholic or anyone who'd like learn more about the A.A. program. Closed meetings are for the alcoholic only.
